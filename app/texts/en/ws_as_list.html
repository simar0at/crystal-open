Collocations as list

This tool will list all collocations found in the corpus in this format %[lempos]% - grammatical relation - lempos. For example: reply-n verbs with "%w" as object write-v It will list the same collocations as the word sketch. The added benefit is that the list can be filtered by the node or the %[collocate]% or the grammatical relation or their part. For example, to list all collocations of negative words starting un-.
