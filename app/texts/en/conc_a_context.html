Filter context

Use the filter for more specific criteria. Only lines matching the criteria will be included in the concordance.

Do not filter

No filter will be applied. All results will be shown.

Lemma context

Only lines containing or not containing certain lemmas will be included. Type one or more lemmas separated by a space. The distance from KWIC and the direction can be specified.

all all lemmas must be present
any at least one of the lemmas must be present
none the line must not contain the lemmas
Part-of-speech context

Only lines containing or not containing certain parts of speech within the distance from KWIC will be included in the concordance. More than one part of speech can be selected.
